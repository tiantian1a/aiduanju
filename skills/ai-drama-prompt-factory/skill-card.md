## Description: <br>
AI Drama Prompt Factory turns Chinese novel text or original story ideas into structured short-drama prompt packages for characters, scenes, props, storyboards, video, and audio. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[zhgarylu](https://clawhub.ai/user/zhgarylu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, developers, and production teams use this skill to convert source fiction or original short-drama ideas into phased planning, design, script, diagnostic, and prompt-package outputs for external generation tools. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Bundled document tools can recursively index and reread broad local file trees if aimed at an overly broad directory. <br>
Mitigation: Run the skill only against a dedicated project folder containing the intended novel or story files, and avoid home directories, full workspaces, cloud-sync folders, secret-bearing repositories, and private business documents. <br>
Risk: Generated indexes may retain summaries or metadata from source story files after the task is complete. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated index files and delete them when they are no longer needed. <br>
